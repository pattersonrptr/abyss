[ ABOUT THE ABYSS ]

ABYSS is an anonymous social experiment.

The idea is simple: you ask a philosophical, existential, ethical question -- anything that keeps spinning in your head -- and other people answer. No name. No profile. No likes. No algorithm deciding who sees what.

No moderation, no reputation to protect, no identity at stake. What remains when we remove all of that? Perhaps more honest thoughts. Perhaps just noise. That is the experiment.

As questions and answers are stored on a server. Nothing is tracked, no identity is recorded.
